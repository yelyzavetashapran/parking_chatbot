Parking Spaces:
Total capacity: 8 spaces.
Available for:
- Cars
- Motorcycles
- Electric vehicles (EV charging available)
We have designated accessible parking spots for people with disabilities.
Oversized vehicle spots available upon request.

Reservation:
You can reserve a parking space in advance by providing:
- Name
- Surname
- Car number
- Reservation start date
- Reservation end date
Reservations can be made via phone, our website, or mobile app.
The payment is conducted directly on the parking location by parking attendant. 
The fee is calculated based on your actual parking time. 
You can stay longer than you reserved if there are no reservations for the lot or other free lots are available at that time.
You can change or cancel your reservation only by calling the administration (public phone number 123456789).
Reservation confirmations are sent via email or SMS.

Safety & Rules:
- No overnight camping or storing personal items.
- Children must be supervised at all times.
- SmartPark is not responsible for lost items inside vehicles.
- Fire lanes must be kept clear at all times.
- Smoking is prohibited in all parking areas.
